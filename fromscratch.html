Pickleball

What you need, rules, and points:

Pickleball Equipment

  • Paddle
  • Pickleball
  • Court shoes

Basic Rules

  • Serve underhand
  • Ball must bounce once on each side
  • No volleying in the kitchen

Scoring

  • Only the serving team scores
  • Games go to 11
  • Must win by 2
